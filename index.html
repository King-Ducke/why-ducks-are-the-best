Why Ducks are the Best

I believe ducks are the best for a number of reasons.

  • Ducks can operate fine on water, land, and in the air.
  • Ducks have exceptional defense mechanisms. All ducks are equipped with an M1 Abrams tank , fully fueled and loaded with M829A4 armor-piercing rounds and High Explosive Anti-Tank rounds. (joke)
  • Ducks are incredibly popular in pop culture, whether it'd be rubber ducks, The Duck Song (peak mention), Donald Duck, or Aflac.

In summary, ducks are the best because they can operate in various environments, they have great (totally real) defense mechanisms, and they are very widespread in pop culture. If you don't like ducks, just know that it's okay to be wrong :].
